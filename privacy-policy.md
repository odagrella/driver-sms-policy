# Privacy Policy

This is a personal SMS notification project for sending daily delivery schedules to a small group of drivers.

## What I collect
Driver phone numbers, which they provided to me directly. Nothing else.

## How I use it
Only to send daily route SMS. The number is stored in a local config file.

## What I do not do
I do not share, sell, or transmit driver phone numbers to any third party. No marketing. No analytics. No tracking.

## Opt-out
Drivers can reply STOP at any time. Their number is removed within 24 hours.

## Contact
For questions or to opt out manually, contact me directly at the phone number on file.
