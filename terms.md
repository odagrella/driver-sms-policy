# Terms of Use ... Driver Run SMS

## Program
Personal SMS service that sends daily delivery routes to participating drivers.

## Messaging frequency
1 to 2 messages per workday (Tuesday through Friday). No messages on weekends or holidays.

## Cost
Message and data rates may apply per your mobile carrier. No fee from the sender.

## How to get help
Reply HELP for assistance.

## How to opt out
Reply STOP to unsubscribe. You will be removed from the daily SMS list.

## Content
Messages contain delivery stops: customer name, address, contact, and ticket number. No marketing.
